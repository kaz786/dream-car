Pagani Huayra

The Pagani Huayra is an Italian mid-engined sports car produced by Pagani.

Features

  • Engine‎: ‎370 cu in (6.0 L) AMG bi-turbo
  • Transmission‎: ‎7-speed sequential
  • Production‎: ‎2012–2018; 2017–present
  • Designer‎: ‎Horacio Pagani
